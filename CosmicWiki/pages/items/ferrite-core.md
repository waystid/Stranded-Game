## Description

Dense metallic nuggets extracted from the island's bedrock. The most common crafting
material — nearly every structure and tool requires Ferrite Cores to build.

## Obtaining

1. **Mineral Node** — hit with Mineral Extractor 6 times (drops 3×)
2. **Chopping trees** — small chance from Xeno-Oak / Xeno-Pine (1× per chop)
3. **Trade Hub** — 50 Stardust each (unlimited stock)

## Visual

Pandazole Survival Crafting Pack small ore/crystal props. Uses `Crystal_01.prefab` as
stand-in model — eventually replace with a dedicated iron nugget mesh.
